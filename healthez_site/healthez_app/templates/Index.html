WHY?

What can I eat? What can kill me? What is this?

Life with allergies can be a matter of life or death when anaphylaxis shock is on the line. For every item that enters your mouth, has to be safe to consume. This means reading the microscopic boxes for the keywords that means life or death to you. As those who live and have such allergies, it's a choir. For those who eyesight is failing, it makes shopping a matter of familiarity and makes exploring new things difficult. That is why we created this site to help those who are facing nutritional problems due to allergies.


WHAT DO WE DO?

Our site helps those with allergies create a nutritionally balanced grocery shopping list that also allows them to check any new items for potential allergens. Simply input the product's name and itll tell you its nutritional and safety information.


WHO WE ARE

We are a team of passionate programmers who wish to aid those like us who face allergen difficulties in our life.
