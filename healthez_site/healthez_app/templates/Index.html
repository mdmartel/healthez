WHY?

What can I eat? What can kill me? What is this?


Life with health conditions, such as allergies, can be a matter of life or death. One has to make sure every item that enters their mouth is safe to consume. This means reading the microscopic cardboard boxes for the allergens, which can be quite the task for some people, such as the elderly. For those whose eyesight is failing, it makes shopping a matter of familiarity and makes exploring new things difficult. The purpose of this site is to help people look-up important nutritional information of food products with ease so they don't have to worry about consuming potentially-dangerous foods.




WHAT DO WE DO?


Our site helps those with health conditions create a nutritionally balanced grocery shopping list by displaying the food product's nutritional information in a clear and concise manner.


There are 2 ways to look-up a food item:


Search up the name: Simply input the item's name (or a category of food) into the search bar and it'll display its nutritional and safety information. You can then add the product to the list.


Upload image: You can upload an image of an item with it's barcode included. You can then add the product to the list.
